Artist

Zoe Djukic was born and raised in Belgrade, Serbia. She currently lives in Charlotte, NC.
Creativity has always fulfilled her need to express herself by way of literature/poetry, acting, photography, and creating various works from her imagination.

She fell in love with softness of pure merino wool in 2007, and immediately started experimenting with the new material and all of its potential forms. Featured in a book title The Art of Soap, Creative Loafing, The Washington Post, Interweave Felt, Surface Design, Lisa Magazine, etc.
